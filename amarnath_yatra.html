Amarnath Yatra

Day 1: Srinagar-Airport-Sonmarg
Arrival and pickup at Srinagar Airport, Drive directly to Pahalgam after 3-4 hours drive you will reach Pahalgam. Afternoon at Pahalgam the valley of shepherds. Dinner & Overnight stay at Hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 2: Sonmarg-Baltal-Panchtharni-Srinagar
After breakfast get ready for Darshan, from Pahalgam take Helicopter ride to Panchtharni. From Panchtharni you can take Pony ride or Palki to Holy cake, after Darshan comeback to Panchtharni and fly back to Pahalgam. Leave Pahalgam and drive towards Srinagar in the late evening you will be at Srinagar, Dinner & Overnight stay at hotel in Srinagar.

Day 3: Day 3: Srinagar– Local sightseeing 50 Kms(Mughal Gardens)
After Breakfast leave for Sonmarg & drive start to Srinagar. Half day tour of world famous Mughal Gardens i.e. Nishat Garden, Shalimar Garden and Cheshma Shahi. In the late Evening Shikara Ride on dal lake Upto 1 Hour. Dinner & Overnight stay at Hotel in Srinagar.

• Nishat Garden
Located on the bank of the Dal Lake, with the Zaberwan Mountains as its backdrop, Nishat Bagh is a garden of bliss that commands a magnificent view of the lake beneath the snow capped Pir Panjal mountain range that stands far away to the west of the valley. The Bagh was designed and built in 1633 by Asif Khan, elder brother of Nur Jehan.

• Shalimar Garden
The Shalimar Bagh is the celebrated royal garden of Kashmir. The Emperor Jahangir was so fascinated with the garden that he called it "one of the sights of Kashmir." Shalimar Bagh is located on the northeast side of Lake Dal, approximately fifteen kilometers away from the center of Srinagar. It was laid out by Jahangir as Farah Baksh ("the delightful") in 1619, while he was founding the city of Srinagar.

• Cheshma Shahi
Cheshma Shahi ( the royal spring), also called Cheshma Shahi, is one of the Mughal gardens built around a spring by Ali Mardan, a governor of Mughal emperor Shah Jahan, in 1632. The garden is located in the Zaberwan Range, near Rajbhawan (Governor‘s house) overlooking Dal Lake in Srinagar, Kashmir.

Day 4: Departure
Transfer in time to the Airport for your onward journey.
